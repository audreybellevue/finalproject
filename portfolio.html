Home Portfolio
Resume

This project asked us to design an online resume that could showcase our background, education, and skills in a clear and accessible way. It was an opportunity to practice formatting, structure, and presentation for a professional digital portfolio.

Climate Change

For our first independent CSS project, we created three linked pages on a cause we cared about. I chose Clean Energy, with the goal of highlighting its importance and the potential consequences of cutting funding and support in the United States.

Wilmette, IL

This project asked us to create a webpage introducing our favorite city or hometown, including four notable sights with links. I chose my hometown, Wilmette, IL, highlighting its history, character, and places that represent what makes it unique.

The Wizard of Oz

This project involved formatting the first three chapters of The Wonderful Wizard of Oz into readable paragraphs across three pages, with an optional introduction. I used CSS to make it both clear to navigate and enjoyable to read.

Red Alert Chili

This project asked us to group content to create an instruction manual for something we care about. I chose my Red Alert Chili and created 4 different sections with images to teach the audience how to make my recipe.
