Resume

This project was to create a resume of our background that could be hosted online.

Climate Change

This project was our first to use CSS independently. We were asked to pick a topic that had to do with a cause we cared about and create three linked pages with descriptions of the issue with images. I chose Clean Energy and the potential consequences for cutting funding and support for its implementation in the United States.

Wilmette, IL

This project asked us to create a page to introduce our favorite city or hometown. We were also asked to include 4 sights in that city and link to them. I chose my hometown, Wilmette, IL as my subject.

The Wonderful Wizard of Oz

This project asked us to take the first 3 chapters of the Wonderful Wizard of Oz and separate them into readable paragraphs on 3 separate pages with an optional introduction page. I had a lot of fun working with CSS to make this both clearly navigable and enjoyable to use

Red Alert Chili

This project asked us to group content to create an instruction manual for something we care about. I chose my Red Alert Chili and created 5 different sections with images to teach the audience how to make my recipe.
